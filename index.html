Reviews for the Krusty Krab

Occasionally mistaken for a lobster trap, the Krusty Krab is a restaurant in the Bikini Bottom. It is famous for its Krabby Patty. It is currently owned by Mr. Krabs, a red crab. A sponge and a squid can be found working there, and a starfish helps from time to time.
